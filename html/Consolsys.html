Consolsys

Consolsys started operations in July of 1999. At Consolsys, we specialize in banking automation solutions.
We are a Malaysian based multi-national company, currently with offices in Malaysia, Philippines and Vietnam.
In 2012, Consolsys appointed JW Consulting with their office located in Jakarta as our local marketing partner
for Indonesia.

Our mission is to provide the Financial Services Industry (FSI) with world class, state-of-the-art industry standards based business automation solutions, superior turnkey project delivery capabilities and responsive support
services.

Our vision is to be "the business automation solution provider of choice for the Financial Services Industry".

Consolsys has a growing team of 100+ highly skilled & experienced staff dedicated to serving our customers. Our senior management team has a combined 180+ years of directly relevant experience in the industry &
individually with no less than 25 years experience gained in both vendor & user environments. We are constantly
looking out for the best minds in the industry to join us.

We are committed to innovation. This commitment is reflected in Consolsys having two subsidiaries dedicated to Research & Development, Consolsys Technologies Sdn Bhd, an MSC Malaysia status company, and Consolsys Islamic Solutions Sdn Bhd, which is focused on Islamic banking solutions R&D.
